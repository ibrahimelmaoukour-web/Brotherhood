# 📜 Code of Conduct\n\n_Brotherhood is a community dedicated to sharing and learning about primitive crafting. We are committed to providing an inclusive, respectful, and welcoming environment for everyone._\n\n## Our Pledge\n\nWe pledge to make participation in the Brotherhood community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of:\n- Age\n- Body size\n- Disability\n- Ethnicity\n- Gender identity or expression\n- Level of experience\n- Nationality\n- Personal appearance\n- Race\n- Religion\n- Sexual identity or orientation\n- Language\n\n## Expected Behavior\n\nAll community members are expected to:\n\n✅ **Be Respectful**\n- Treat all community members with kindness\n- Value diverse perspectives and backgrounds\n- Listen to others with an open mind\n\n✅ **Be Honest**\n- Acknowledge mistakes and learn from them\n- Give and receive constructive feedback gracefully\n- Research thoroughly before contributing\n\n✅ **Be Inclusive**\n- Welcome newcomers warmly\n- Help others learn and grow\n- Make space for quieter voices\n\n✅ **Be Collaborative**\n- Focus on the common goal: building knowledge\n- Help fellow contributors improve their work\n- Share knowledge freely\n\n✅ **Be Professional**\n- Use appropriate language\n- Maintain courtesy in all interactions\n- Stay focused on the work\n\n## Unacceptable Behavior\n\nThe following behaviors are not tolerated:\n\n❌ **Harassment**\n- Unwelcome comments about someone's identity\n- Threats or intimidation\n- Deliberate exclusion\n- Persistent unwanted attention\n\n❌ **Hate Speech**\n- Slurs or derogatory language\n- Content that demeans any group\n- Discrimination of any kind\n\n❌ **Disruptive Behavior**\n- Spamming or trolling\n- Off-topic derailment of discussions\n- Bad-faith arguments\n\n❌ **Unethical Conduct**\n- Plagiarism or taking credit for others' work\n- Spreading misinformation\n- Spam or advertising\n- Copyright violations\n\n## Consequences\n\nUnacceptable behavior will result in:\n\n1. **First Offense**: Private warning with explanation\n2. **Second Offense**: Temporary suspension from participation\n3. **Third Offense**: Permanent removal from the community\n\nMaintainers reserve the right to escalate consequences for severe violations.\n\n## Reporting\n\nIf you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:\n\n1. **Document** what happened (message, screenshot, date/time)\n2. **Contact** the maintainers privately:\n   - Open a private issue\n   - Email maintainers directly\n   - Do not call out publicly first\n\n3. **Investigation**: Maintainers will investigate promptly and confidentially\n\n4. **Resolution**: We'll work toward a fair resolution\n\nAll reports will be treated seriously and confidentially.\n\n## Scope\n\nThis Code of Conduct applies to:\n- GitHub Issues and Pull Requests\n- Code reviews and comments\n- Community discussions\n- All Brotherhood spaces\n\n## Attribution\n\nThis Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.0.\n\n## Questions?\n\nIf you have questions about this Code of Conduct, please ask in an issue or contact the maintainers.\n\n---\n\n**Together, we build a respectful and welcoming community.**
